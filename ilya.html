Guild of Heroes: Pathfinder Corp

In the Guild's early days, quests were usually limited to rumors or whether commoners could reach front desk fast enough. This wasn't an issue at the beginning, as the guild only had so much manpower in the chaotic days of old. But as the guild grew with more members and influence, so too did misinformation, fraud, and lack of trust around various quest givers. Quests started to report mismatching information and rewards due to a variety of reasons: commoner lack of knowledge, greedy merchants, villainous individuals, and even just no one being in the area to confirm. The Guild attempted to mitigate this through an informal system of familiars, tools, and magic, but it wasn't enough. The Wolford Stars Incident and subsequent Twilight Battle of Wolford forced the Guild to acknowledge the their lack of information gathering and processing capabilities. As such, the Pathfinder Corp was established.

The Pathfinder Corp was formed to cover the roles of intelligence gathering, quest verification, and regular scouting duties, however has since expanded to include exploring regions where guildmembers, and people in general, don't usually reach. Acting as the Guild's eyes and ears on the ground, pathfinders explore areas where the Guild can't through conventional means, gathering accurate information about possible quests and threats. They also act as a psuedo intelligence network in conjunction with various mercantile guilds.

Pathfinders exist within the same hierarchy of the Guild, but are afforded more privileges and trust in exchange for stricter requirements and duties. In essence, they act as mobile guild staff, able to officiate in matters corresponding to their rank. They also carry specialized items including observation tools, priority communication tools, and evacuation equipment. At minimum pathfinders must be able to fight and survive long enough to deliver information back to the Guild. Usually they will stay onsite to act as guides or informants for guild members who follow. There are also pathfinders who specialize in espionage activities, gathering information on nations and organizations hostile to the guild. But most pathfinders explore less traveled and unknown regions.

For guild members, they are the guides and quest givers. For hostile forces, they are the encroaching signs of heroes. But for the many who despair, they are first signs of hope, the hope that the Heroes will come.

Skills & Abilities
Avant Elf (City Elf)
At home in urban settings. Accustomed to human traditions and culture, she is infinitely better than the country bumpkin that just left starter village 1. Capable of both Common and Elven languages.
Valoran, Espanian
From the city of Valora, Espania. Close ties to Espanians, but also due to Valora's proximity to Albion, she is familiar with Albion culture.
Runefencer
Skilled in fencing. While she knows the basics to sword fighting, her style is that of a fencer, using the side-sword (Spada da lato). Her technique and skill, while not on the level of a master, are refined to the point where she can effectively stand her ground against multiple opponents.
Adorable Demeanor
Her petite form, her adorable face, and her soft expressions have disarmed even the most rugged of people. She easily makes friends with her adorable animal like actions. Many have liken her to a small animal that must be protect at all costs.
Magic
Spirit Magic
As an Elf, this comes naturally to her, or rather should. Due to her childhood of being an Avant Elf, her contact with powerful spirits are little to none, and she can only sense spirits and spirit magic. Her only real application of Spirit Magic is enhancing her senses momentarily.
Runes
Engraved onto her sword and armor are a multitude of runes. She can also create runes herself. These runes can be active as she wills with some mana, but only one for each piece it is on,
Rune - Flame (sword)
Engulfs her sword in flames.
Rune - Ice (sword)
Sword attacks are so cold that they cause frostbite to unprotected parts of the body.
Rune - Wind (sword)
Surrounds her sword in wind magic, making it more agile and faster, with increased piercing potential.
Rune - Light (chestpiece)
Applies a small regeneration, capable only of healing minor cuts and scratches.
Rune - Air (chestpiece)
Lightens her body, making her more agile and faster.
Rune - Dark (knife)
Places which are hit by her knife start to corrode with multiple hits. Doesn't work on flesh.
